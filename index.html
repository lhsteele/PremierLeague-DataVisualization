English Premier League
The Premier League is the top level of the English football (soccer) system in England, made up of 20 clubs.
Each club plays each other twice, at home and away, totalling 38 matches throughout the season.
At the end of the season, the club with the most points wins the Premier League title.
The top 4 clubs win a place to compete in the UEFA Champions League, and the bottom 3 clubs are relegated to the First Division.
